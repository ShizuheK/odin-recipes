Lemon Mousse Recipe

Description

A simple dessert, quick to make and very tasty

Instructions and images from BBC Good Food

Ingredients

  • 300g jar good-quality lemon curd
  • 1 lemon
  • 300ml pot whipping or double cream
  • 25g cruchy lemon flavoured biscuits (optional toppings)

Steps

  1. Put two-thirds of the lemon curd into a large bowl with the zest and cream, then beat with an electric whisk until it just holds its shape. Dribble over the rest of the lemon curd and divide between 6 small glass pots or glasses, marbling the curd through as you go. Sit the pots on a small tray, cover the whole lot with cling film and freeze for 30-40 minutes.
  2. (Optional toppings) Put the biscuits in a strong plastic bag and bash with the end of a rolling pin to crumbs. When you're ready for pudding, remove the pots from the freezer (they should be ice cold but still soft and creamy), top with crumbs and serve.
